// 04 — architecture expertise

The Medallion pattern, end to end

A disciplined Bronze → Silver → Gold foundation for governance, data quality and analytics readiness — refining every dataset for its intended business use.

Br
Bronze
raw / source of truth

Ingests raw structured and unstructured data from diverse sources through ADF and DLT pipelines — preserving lineage, traceability and the original source of truth for downstream processing.

▸
Ag
Silver
cleansed / conformed

Transforms raw inputs into cleansed, validated, conformed datasets — applying quality controls, standardisation and Unity Catalog governance to create trusted, reusable data assets.

▸
Au
Gold
analytics-ready

Delivers curated, aggregated, analytics-ready data products optimised for business consumption — powering Power BI, self-service reporting and decision-making with speed and confidence.
